Quality of Life Skills

Planning(Pseudocode)

When given a prompt or an idea, plan out what you are going to do in the future. Form a 'map' for your creation.


Collaboration

Collaborating with a partner(s) is a great experience not only to work efficiently, but learn from their thinking.


Communication

Communication with both your peers and teacher is much needed. If in any case you are not understanding something, they are there to help. Doing the same for them is also a great way to build trust.


Independent Learning

Laerning on your own will take its toll as more difficult, however will give you a sharper edge in the understanding of coding.


Presenting

In this class, you will have to present your creation in one way or another. Be prepared to share out your process of getting to where you are now.


Punctuality

Submitting your assignments in on time bears no negatives. It will serve as a great habit for the future, and boost your grades.


Deadlines

Make sure to always be able to meet the deadline with what you're doing. As a wise man once said, 'Done is better than perfect.'


Practicing/learning outside of class

Opportunities for coding will appear from time to time that are outside of class, take advantage of them and participate if you are interested. There are also many other websites that can aid with your knowledge of coding in the web.


Resources you can use:

Whiteboarding - pseudocode
gDoc/gDrawing - planning on goodle drive, etc
w3schools - a helpful site that can answer many of your questions about coding
